# Campaign Kit — Thumbnail Concept

## Generation Prompt

```
A dynamic creative collage on a warm dark background (#1A0F14). 
Three content panels arranged in a diagonal cascade: 
Top-left: a glowing video player frame showing a vibrant lifestyle scene with play button overlay.
Center-right: a stylized product photograph with dramatic rim lighting against a gradient backdrop.
Bottom-left: a bold typography/text ad panel with sleek sans-serif headline treatment.
Each panel has a subtle paper-tear edge or polaroid-style white border. 
Scattered creative tools around the edges: a stylized pen tip, a color palette swatch, a camera lens silhouette. 
Vibrant coral (#FF6B6B), warm gold (#F7C948), and electric teal (#00D4AA) accent colors throughout.
Soft bokeh particles in the background. 
No readable text in the main image — only abstract text lines in the ad panel. 
Studio lighting, 3D depth, energetic but not chaotic composition, professional creative studio vibe.
```

## Color Palette

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Background | `#1A0F14` | Deep warm charcoal — studio darkness |
| Primary accent | `#FF6B6B` | Coral red — energy, CTA feel |
| Secondary accent | `#F7C948` | Warm gold — highlights, premium feel |
| Tertiary accent | `#00D4AA` | Electric teal — contrast, freshness |
| Panel border | `#FFFFFF` | Clean white polaroid edges |
| Glow | `#FF8E53` | Soft warm glow from bottom-right |

## Style Notes

- **Mood**: Energetic, creative, polished — like an ad agency mood board
- **Composition**: Diagonal cascade (three panels flowing top-left to bottom-right)
- **Depth**: 3D layering — panels overlap with subtle drop shadows and Z-offset
- **Lighting**: Warm studio rim light, subtle lens flare in upper-left
- **Texture**: Light paper/canvas texture on panels, clean where tools are
- **Typography**: Only abstract text lines (gray rectangles) — no real words
- **Detail level**: Rich — this benefits from more detail since it's a creative tool

## Composition Guidance

- Canvas: 1024x1024 square
- Panel 1 (video): ~380x280px, top-left quadrant, tilted -3°
- Panel 2 (product): ~320x400px, center-right, slightly overlapping Panel 1
- Panel 3 (text ad): ~340x200px, bottom-left, completing the triangle
- Creative tools: Scattered in negative space (top-right corner, bottom-right)
- Play button: Small white triangle overlay on video panel (15% opacity circle behind)
- Color swatches: 3 tiny circles clustered near bottom-right
- Focus: Center of the composition where all three panels converge
- Spacing: Panels cover ~75% of canvas with breathing room

## Output Spec

- **Format**: WebP
- **Dimensions**: 1024x1024
- **Intent**: Catalog avatar / thumbnail grid item
- **Style taxonomy**: Creative / collage / marketing / studio / vibrant
